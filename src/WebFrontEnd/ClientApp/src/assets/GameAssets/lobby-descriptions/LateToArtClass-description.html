Overview

Art class has started and you have no idea what topic was assigned. Take a peak at your neighbor's artwork and guess the assigned topic.

Goal

If you are late to class, do your best to guess the assignment so your artwork blends in with the class.

Gameplay

You will draw several images. Sometimes you will be given the topic, sometimes you will be late and just see what someone else drew. Do not simply recreate the other drawing or everyone will know that you copied.

When all the artwork is displayed on the viewer, try to guess which artist was late to art class.
