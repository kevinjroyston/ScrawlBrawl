Late To Art Class

Stage 1 - Drawing Prompts

You will enter some art class drawing prompts be prompted to enter actions and characters.

Stage 2 - Draw

You will either receive a drawing prompt, or you will be "late to class" and have missed the prompt. You'll have to copy off of your neighbor's artwork.

Stage 3 - Find the Latecomer

Look at the works of art and determine who showed up late for class and copied off their neighbor.

Points

You get points if no one notices you were late to class.
