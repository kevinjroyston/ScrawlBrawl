Late To Art Class

Art class has started and you have no idea what topic was assigned. Take a peak at your neighbor's artwork and guess the assigned topic.

Stage 1 - Drawing Prompts

You will enter some art class drawing topics.

Stage 2 - Draw

You will either receive a drawing prompt, or you will be "late to class" and have missed the prompt.
If you're late, you'll have to copy off of your neighbor's artwork. But don't simply recreate their image or everyone will know you copied. Try to determine the topic and draw your own image

Stage 3 - Find the Latecomer

Look at the works of art and determine who showed up late for class and copied off their neighbor.

Points

You gain points if you can spot the late student (partial credit if you find who they copied from).

You lose out on some points if people incorrectly think you were the late student.
