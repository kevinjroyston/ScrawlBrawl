Imposter Syndrome

You are going to ask everyone to draw a picture, but you are going to create a special prompt for one random player (the Imposter).  The goal is to come up with a pair of prompts where you can tell which drawing was the imposter, but no one else can.

Stage 1 - Prompt Creation

You will be asked for two prompts: one for everyone and one for the imposter. If you are confused, simply hit the SUGGEST button!

Examples of good prompts

Prompt for Everyone Prompt for the Imposter
Olympic Games Javelin Toss
Baseball Game Homerun

Examples of BAD prompts (too easy to spot the imposter)

Prompt for Everyone Prompt for the Imposter
Racecar Pickup Truck
Basket of fruit Banana

Stage 2 - Drawing

You will be asked to draw something... so, draw it.   It doesn't have to be a masterpiece, just get the idea across.

Be careful not to talk too much during the drawing round or else you may divulge crucial information to the other players.

Stage 3 - Voting

Take a close look at all the drawings.  Remember what your drawing prompt was... do they all fit that pattern?     Choose the drawing you think was the imposter. But be careful, YOU might be the imposter.

 

Points

Everyone earns points for correctly picking out the imposter.

You also earn points for blending in.  Every vote for someone else gives you a few points.
