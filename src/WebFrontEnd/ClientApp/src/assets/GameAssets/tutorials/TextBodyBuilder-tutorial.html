Character Builder

Create your champion from a collection of characters and actions, then add your own modifier to give it a clever twist.

Stage 1 - Characters and Actions

You will be prompted to enter actions and characters.

Tip: Hit the SUGGEST button on top to cycle through some suggested choices.

Stage 2 - Scenarios

This is a battle of champions.  You create the scenarios. 

"World's best dog walker"
"Least likely to succeed"
"Intergalactic Space Hero"

Tip: Hit the SUGGEST button on top to cycle through some suggested scenarios.

Stage 3 - Build Your Character

It's time to assemble your champion.   Look at the scenario and select from the provided characters and actions to create your hero.

Stage 4 - Add Your Modifier

Sure you chose a character and an action, but now you can add your own modifier to paint an epically vivid picture.

Stage 5 - Voting

Look the shared game viewer to see the champions vying for the title.  Vote on your device.

Points

You get points if your champion receives votes.

You also earn points if other players use your brilliant words.
