Chaotic Collaboration

It's time to work with your team to create a masterful work of art.

Who's on your team???? NO IDEA!

It's time to buddy up and create a stunning work of art.

Who is your partner???? WHO KNOWS????? (That's part of the "Chaos")

Stage 1 - What to Draw

Enter a title for your masterpiece.

Describe the foreground and the background of your drawing.

Describe each layer of your drawing (foreground, middle, background, etc.).

Select the color for each layer of your drawing. Remember they can only draw with this color, they will have to figure out what to draw based on the title and the color they are assigned.

Keep in mind what what you tell them to draw in the foreground will be drawn ON TOP of the other layers. (Things in the background will be painted over by anything on the top layers.)

Stage 2 - Draw

Read the title of the artwork you are going to create.

Pay close attention to the different layers of the drawing. Notice which one is assigned to you and whether you are drawing on top or below the other artists.

Stage 3 - Voting

Everyone's drawings will appear on the shared game viewer. Use your device to vote for the best drawing.

Points

You receive points if you vote for the most popular drawing.

You also earn points if other players pick your collaboration as their favorite drawing.
