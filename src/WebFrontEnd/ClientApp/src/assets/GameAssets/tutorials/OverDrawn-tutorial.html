Over-Drawn

Overview

You are commissioned to create a masterpiece. Unfortunately, your apprentice misheard your request and has started painting the wrong thing.

Stage 1 - Draw commissions

Given a prompt, craft your perfect art piece.

Stage 2 - Draw over commissions

Given a new prompt, compete against the other players in drawing over the existing drawing to see who can best fulfill the new prompt.

Stage 3 - Voting

Pick who best tweaked the original drawing to fulfill the new requirements.

Points

You get points if your submission receives votes from other players.

You also earn a few points if your vote matches that of the majority.
